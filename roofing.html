Roof Repairs

Damaged or leaking roof? No problem. Our experienced and professional repair crews are able to service and repair all types of residential roofing.

Don't put off roof repairs

It's important to take care of your roof throughout its lifetime so it protects the rest of your home for years to come. Level Up Construction TC LLC completes roof repairs quickly and efficiently to make sure your roof is in tip-top shape. A roof replacement can be a big investment, so take proper care of the one you have and it can last for 20-25 years.

Roof Replacement

Time to replace your existing roof? We are knowledgeable about the latest in roofing products and can install a wide range of premium roofing materials.

Signs that you need a new roof

  • Its shingles are curling, buckling, missing or falling apart.
  • Your gutters are loaded with shingle material.
  • Daylight is showing through the floorboards.
  • The roof feels spongy.
